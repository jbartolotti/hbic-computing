HBIC Computing
===================================

Hoglund computing resources include:

* A primary research server: **hbic-synapse**
* Two secondary, lightweight research servers: **hbic-alpha** and **hbic-beta**
* Two lightweight compute servers: **hbic-gamma** and **hbic-delta**
* `XNAT <https://xnat.kumc.edu>`_ : web-based neuroimaging data management
